FELICIA


Felicia Kids home was established in 2005. Felicia Kids Home is an orphanage providing a safe home to the orphans since October 2005.There are 33 kids, 16 boys and 17 girls from the age of 5yrs to 18years. The children are taken care so that they can receive love and training that enable them to be productive, independent and be the future leaders of the local community.The Vision is to provides a home environment where the children feel accepted, loved and secured. Felicia Kids Home targets boys and girls from the age of five, regardless of race or religious background.
