PEACE HOME

Peace Home- Beth Raphe is orphanage located in Hubli. This orphanage has 20 children.It supports the children for their basic needs and with a place safe to stay. The orphanage help the students in achieving their goals of life. Many cultural activities, physical education sessions, counsellings have been held so far to maintain the peace minds of the children.
