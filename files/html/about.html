About us

We the students of Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwar college of engineering and technology, Dharwad developed this website as a part of curriculum project to ensure healthy and conducive environment to all the children. Twin City Orphanages was built to donate assist and empower all the orphanages across Hubli Dharwad. People tend to donate to the orphanages that are known or near by which leads to lack of resources for other orphanages that are not well known. This website enables the user to donate to the right and needy orphanage. We have created this website to ensure proper functioning of NGOS which leads to healthy environment and betterment of all the children.
