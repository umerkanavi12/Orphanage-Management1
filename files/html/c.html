DAYASHANKAR GURUKULA


Dayashankar Gurukul is a orphanage located at Dharwad.This orphanage is only for boys. There are currently 47 boys. It intensively strives to give good education, food and shelter. The children are practiced with the morning prayers, helping others, maintaining cleanliness. The students study in the nearby government schools.
