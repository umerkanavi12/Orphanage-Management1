SEVABHARATI


Sevabharathi(Social Service Organization) is a orphanage home for women.It provides shelter home,education for girls.There are 37 girls in this orphanages from the age of 0 to 20 years. It aims to give good education and most importantly empower them and be self-reliant. Medical Camps are done once in 3 months.Awareness programs have been conducted based on Health and cleanliness. Many cultural activities will be conducted.
