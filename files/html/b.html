SUSHANTI

Sushanti Children Home is non-profit organzation located in Hubli.The organization is working for children for the past 16 years. There are 37 children, 27 girls and 10 boys in the age group of 6 to 16. The goal of the Sushanti Children Home is to continue to make a difference in the lives of Orphan, Semi-Orphan, Destitute and Poor Children with the help of donations from supporters such that you will continue to see improvements in children's lives. This is not supported by any Government aid, Foreign donation or such organizational support. Trust runs the orphanage on the support of well-wishers. The main motto is to aid as many underprivileged children as possible and to get them educated for responsible citizens of India. Counselling is done on healthcare, awareness program was given by Dr.Jessica and group. Many cultural programs have been conducted.
